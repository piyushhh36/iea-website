Reading Club

Read slowly, question deeply and speak with precision.

Reading Club helps learners engage with meaningful texts through vocabulary, context, key lines, summaries, short answers and reflective discussion.

Join Reading ClubAll Clubs

Session Design

A serious but engaging format.

A text is not rushed. Participants read, pause, interpret, question, disagree and connect the passage with their own life.

Lead-in

Short personal question, poll or image prompt.

Input

Video, text, story, extract, film scene or quote.

Activity

Pair work, group discussion, movement task or reflection.

Share

Participants speak, listen, question and refine expression.

Reflect & Share

Questions that bring language close to life.

01

Which line from the text feels uncomfortable but true?

02

What did you understand only after reading slowly?

03

Which word or phrase changed the way you saw the passage?

04

Where does the text expose something in ordinary life?
